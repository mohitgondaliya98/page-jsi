Who We Are

Page JSI Company is the joint venture between Page Europa and Jana Marine Services

Page JSI company born from the merger of two major players, to deal with the challenges of the Energy and Critical Infrastructure sector with sophisticated, comprehensive and localized telecom and security solutions.

Page Europa has entered into a strategic partnership with Jana Marine Services Company LLC, a Saudi company wholly owned by the Rashed Al-Rashed & Sons group, founded in 2013 and active in the maritime logistics services sector serving the offshore Oil & Gas industry.

About Us

Page JSI offers solution for the telecom and security systems

Page JSI is the JV between Page Europa and Jana Marine Services, with the mission to deliver integrated telecommunications, cybersecurity and surveillance systems operating seamlessly together, enhancing operations, safety, and efficiency. Our involvement is critical from the planning and design phases through to the implementation, maintenance and upgrading of legacy telecom systems.

Page JSI offers HCIS compliant solutions for the telecom and security systems for the industry, including Oil & Gas platforms, substations, renewable energy plants or any large infrastructures such as ports, airports and data centers. Our capabilities enable a turn-key project delivery approach, from the front-end engineering to the design, procurement, integration, testing, on-site commissioning and maintenance.
